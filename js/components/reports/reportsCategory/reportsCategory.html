Helpful Tip

Note: Did you know that Artecsan converts all your units sold to their unit of delivery. For example, let’s say you order 100lbs of brisket which you serve in your tacos (6oz of brisket per taco). Now let’s say that your POS records 10 brisket tacos yesterday. When you view Artecsan sales report, instead of seeing “10 brisket tacos” sold, you will instead see 3.75lbs of brisket plus all the other ingredients.
